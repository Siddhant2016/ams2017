ABOUT US

ABOUT THE INSTITUTE

The Indian Institute of Information Technology was conferred the Deemed University" status by Govt. of India in the year 2000.The Institute has been conceived with the ambitious objectives of developing professional expertise and skilled manpower in Information Technology (IT) and related areas.

As an apex nucleating institute in the area of IT, the establishment of IIIT-A, is a major step of Govt. of India towards strengthening the indigenous capability necessary for exploiting profitably and harnessing multi-dimensional facets of IT at all levels, and attaining expertise to enable the country to emerge as a leading player in the global arena

ABOUT THE SOCIETY

Thunderbolt or the Acoustics and Media Society (AMS) as it is commonly known is the student crew that overlooks the lights, audio and media related engagements of all academic and cultural gallerys organized at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad.

Thunderbolt operates under the ambit of the cultural council of the Gymkhana, IIIT Allahabad. The 18 member team works on war front to ensure that all gallerys conducted by other societies at IIITA are given due coverage and proper records and pictures are maintained in our database.
